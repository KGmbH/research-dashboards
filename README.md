# Research Dashboards

A collection of interactive dashboards built with Quarto and R to present research findings in a clear and accessible way.

🔗 **Live site:** https://kgmbh.github.io/research-dashboards/

---

## Dashboards

### Wh-Exclamatives  
[Open dashboard](./wh_exclamatives_dashboard/index.html)  
Experimental investigation of felicity conditions and interpretation of wh-exclamative clauses.

---

### Gender Slur Dashboard  
[Open dashboard](./gender_slur_dashboard/index.html)  
Explores how gender-oriented derogation varies with context, speaker properties, and interpretation across experimental studies.

---

## About

These dashboards are based on my research in experimental linguistics and pragmatics.  
They combine:

- experimental design (forced-choice, acceptability, reaction time)
- statistical modeling (e.g., mixed-effects models in R)
- data visualization and interactive presentation

---

## Tools

- R (tidyverse, ggplot2, lme4, etc.)
- Quarto (interactive dashboards)
